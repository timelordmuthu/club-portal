FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who can participate in the hackathon? ‚ñº

All students from any department and year are welcome! Whether you're a coding wizard or a curious beginner, we encourage everyone to participate and learn.

Do I need to be in a team? ‚ñº

You can participate solo or form teams of up to 4 members. Don't have a team? No worries! We'll help you find teammates during the opening ceremony.

What should I bring to the event? ‚ñº

Bring your laptop, chargers, student ID, and your creative mind! We'll provide food, drinks, WiFi, and all the energy you need for 48 hours of hacking.

Is there any registration fee? ‚ñº

No! Tech Horizon 2026 is completely free to attend. Just register, show up, and hack away!

What if I'm a beginner with no experience? ‚ñº

Perfect! We have workshops and mentors specifically to help beginners. This is the best place to learn by doing. Everyone starts somewhere!

Will food and accommodation be provided? ‚ñº

Yes! We'll provide meals, snacks, and beverages throughout the 48 hours. Accommodation arrangements can be discussed with the organizing team.
